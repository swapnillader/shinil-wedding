Our Story

How a Healer met the Innovator

Rapunzel - The Healer

She is the one who heals beyond what medicine can touch. With her presence, she calms storms I didn’t even know I had. Her love has this quiet strength—it doesn’t just support me, it restores me. She captured my heart without trying, and somehow, made it feel like home. I’ve watched her pour herself out—at work, in worship, in every little moment in between—with a grace that amazes me every day. She’s one of the most hardworking people I know, but it’s her heart that truly sets her apart. She’s endlessly kind, deeply compassionate, and always ready to show up for others—whether it’s a whispered prayer, a helping hand, or simply a warm smile. Her love for God is the kind that changes atmospheres. She doesn’t just worship on a stage—she lives it, breathes it, and brings it into everything she does. And through her, I’ve come closer to God than ever before. To know her is to see a glimpse of the Father’s heart. And to walk beside her? That’s a gift I’ll never stop thanking God for.

Eugene - The Innovator

He moves through the world not just seeing what is, but envisioning what could be, his mind a vibrant forge of ideas and possibilities & love & care for everyone ...he is the one who sloved all my problems through his creative , funny & lovely ideas!! It's in the quiet moments, watching him in excited spark in his eyes when he finally cracks a solution, that I truly grasp the depth of his innovative spirit. He doesn't just build or create; he breathes life into concepts, transforming the abstract into something tangible and often, something beautiful. Just like he did in my life bring lots of happiness and hope that directed from lord. His unwavering belief in progress, coupled with a relentless drive to improve and invent, isn't just a part of who he is—it's a light that illuminates our shared journey, inspiring me every single day. He is godly man who always try to find the happiness and spread lord's love everywhere he go..a wonderful inovator of my life who slove all the problems of mine.

How We Connected

We were two worshippers walking our own journeys, seeking not love, but more of God. It was in that sacred space—where hearts are aligned with heaven—that we found each other. Not in a crowd, not by chance, but through divine orchestration. One brings healing with her hands and her heart. The other builds and dreams in lines of code and vision. Together, we found more than compatibility—we discovered a calling. Through prayer, laughter, worship, and many coffee-fueled conversations, our bond grew. We didn’t just fall in love; we were led into it. Every moment, every step, was gently guided by the One who authors all love stories. This is not just our story—it’s a testimony. A journey of faith, of grace, and of two lives intertwining under the banner of His purpose. And now, we step into a new chapter—together.
